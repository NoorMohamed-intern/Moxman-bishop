About Us

Bishops Scientific is one of India’s leading companies working in the field of Sales, distribution and service of Laboratory Instrumentation, accessories, and consumables. Bishops works with premier lab technology companies from around the world as their India partner to help bring these innovative technologies and products to the Indian customers.

The promoters and management team of Bishops Scientific – the Parekh family and the Shah family have a combined experience of more than 60 years in the Scientific and Analytical industry in India.
